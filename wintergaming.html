"Its winter and its game time"

2020 has been a pretty rough year so far, and with the winter approaching, chances of catching a cold has increased. However whats in our favour are the amazing game releases made lately. Q4 of 2020 has been great for gamers and with game releases such as Assassin's Creed Valhalla, Watch Dogs Legion, Call of Duty Black Ops Cold War, its just the perfect time to game. Keeping it short and simple, here are my top recommendations for a decent gaming experience while stuck at home. Enjoy!
